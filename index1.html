What is AndroidBuzz ?

AndroidBuzz is an android app development hackathon organized by the Google Developer Student Club, Thakur College of Engineering and Technology

It is a virtual hackathon that starts on 16th December 2021

Problem Statement

College or University App

An App that could help your fellow students or faculty or college. Some suggestions here could be, timetable tracking app, Attendance recording app, University transportation tracking app, University navigation app, University database for different departments, etc.

Community App

An app that can help a smaller or larger community around you. Identify a community-level problem around you and try to build a solution for it using an app. Here are a few ideas, Free Food centers app, Free E-learning resources app, etc.

Private or Individual Solution App

An app that can change an individual's life or whole business performance. We would encourage you to go to small local business owners e.g. Bakery Shop, Gyms Centers, Restaurants, or any other local; businesses to understand their business model and identify a problem that can be solved by using an app.

Rules

One-pager intro in ReadMe which contains:


  • Problem Statement
  • Proposed solution with screenshot
  • functionality and concepts use
  • application link and future scope

The app should contain :


  • Kotlin as the primary language
  • Use android Jetpack libraries
  • At least 2-3 screens should be there
  • Make use of Live data, Viewmodel and Room Database
  • (optional) connect your app through the internet.
